Our Places

Saladhamma

Saladhamma is another most memorable and respected place in CMU which inside is the located the Buddha statue for CMU.

AngKaew

AngKeaw is currently the only lake to store the water to supply Chiangmai University. It is located near the front gate of the university. Not only store the water to supply the university but Angkeaw lake is also one of the most memorable spot in Chiangmai university due to it magnificent view of Suthep mountain.

CMU Cock Tower

The CMU clock tower is considered to be an important historical landmark of Chiang Mai university. It located in the middle of a street roundabout in front of engineering faculty. With the astounding view of Suthep mountain with Pra tat doi Suthep on visual makes it one of the most beautiful landmarks of CMU.
